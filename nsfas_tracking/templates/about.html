MISSION STATEMENT

With the battle of a pandemic, the world has changed. With our country having to fast track the 4th industrial revolution introduced by the precident, each organization and institutions have moved to doing their daily business online. Many institutions introduced online learning in order to continue teaching and learning, but many students are reluctant to attent this online classes

Our mission statement is to save money that NSFAS invest in each student and to encourage students to attend online classes as they are important as those that are campus based

SYSTEM REQUIREMENT

The system will help the NSFAS department keep track of students, whether they are attending classes or not, In doing so the student will have to log into the system. upon log in the student will find an MS Team link that would have been posted by the lecture subsequently that the student can attend the class online.

The NSFAS office on their page they will be able to view all the students who attended all the sissions that a lecturer posted. Time(duration in which student attended the lesson) and date will be recorded.

The System will generate the report so that the NSFAS office can be able to see the activities of each student and overal attendence of online classes.
